FSA & HSA for Glasses: Everything You Need to Know.

Can I use my FSA/HSA for glasses?

Yes! Prescription glasses and sunglasses are eligible FSA/HSA expenses. All products on 39dollarglasses.com automatically qualify when you have a valid prescription.

How does Flex Payments work?

Flex Payments is our seamless checkout system that instantly verifies your FSA/HSA card. Simply enter your card details at checkout like any regular purchase – no additional steps, forms, or waiting for approval.

Are prescription sunglasses covered?

Absolutely! Prescription sunglasses are fully FSA/HSA eligible. Protect your eyes from UV rays while using your pre-tax dollars.

Do I need to submit receipts?

No! With Flex Payments, everything is handled automatically at checkout. We'll email you a detailed receipt for your records, but you won't need to submit any paperwork to your FSA/HSA administrator.
